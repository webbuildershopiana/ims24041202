WELCOME MESSAGE
Dear Friends/colleagues,
On behalf of the organizing team, it is our sheer pleasure to welcome you to 2nd Edition of International Musculoskeletal Summit (IMS) to be held in the vibrant city of Dubai, where every moment shines. The first edition of IMS which took place in Mauritius (15-17th September 2023) was a grand success with raving reviews from all delegates, trade partners and faculties and was attended by delegates from 15 different countries. The event was attended by the health minister of Mauritius and broadcasted on national television.
This year the course is aimed at beginners while also covering intermediate level and some advanced topics in Musculoskeletal Radiology/Medicine. The delegates will be welcomed with a meet and greet event on Friday evening and beach side gala dinner accompanied with cultural events on Saturday night. The conference Venue is a luxury 5 star hotel in Dubai.  
WHEN
MUSCULOSKELETAL ULTRASOUND WORKSHOP
Workshop aimed at delegates with Beginners to intermediate level exposure to Musculoskeletal Ultrasound with 20 percent content for Advanced level.
❖ Introduction to Musculoskeletal Ultrasound – Basic theory and Knobology
❖ 7:1 Delegates: Faculty ratio during the hands-on sessions
❖ Lectures on anatomy and pathology before Individual joint hands-on sessions
❖ High-end Ultrasound machines with linear probes available during the entire course for practice of delegates
MUSCULOSKELETAL MRI INTERACTIVE LECTURES
Global Musculoskeletal MRI Experts will be delivering interactive Lectures on Musculoskeletal MRI
❖ Technical considerations for Optimizing MSK MRI
❖ MRI in Degenerative Joint diseases
❖ MRI spine including seronegative Spondyloarthropathies
❖ MRI in Sports medicine/Sports Injuries
MUSCULOSKELETAL INTERVENTIONS
Basic to intermediate Introduction to the science of MSK Interventions and a special live demo preparation of Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP)
❖ Basics of Ultrasound Guided MSK Interventions
❖ Workshop on Orthobiologics Preparation
❖ Interactive Lectures and sessions on various MSK Interventions
